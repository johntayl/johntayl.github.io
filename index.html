About

Websites, apps, games, technology. It's what brings us together in an ever changing world.

Growing up with access to computers and gaming consoles, I was always surrounded by the latest tech.I wanted to explore and understand how these things were made. In my youth, I dabbled in making basic websites on the long forgotten GeoCities, but it was the place which got me started. RPGMaker was up next which introduced me into game development. Learning how to layout a 2D tiled environment and add basic scripts and events. It was my first programming experience, while not knowing what programming was.

These experiences brought me down a path of studying computer science throughout high school and university, then venturing into business programming in college. The combination of computer science and business are what drive my goals today.

I am a Software Developer in Toronto, Canada that loves to collaborate on building projects and companies. See my previous work history on LinkedIn. If you would like to collaborate on interesting ideas or projects, feel free to reach out.
